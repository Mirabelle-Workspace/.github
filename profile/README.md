# Mirabelle Workspace

A curated home for Mirabelle’s case studies, experiments, and build-in-public work.

## What this organization is

Mirabelle Workspace is the umbrella for projects that document how ideas move from concept to execution. It brings together case studies, prototypes, and working repos in one place so visitors can quickly understand the scope of the work and explore it in depth.

## What you’ll find here

- **Case studies** that break down strategy, process, and outcomes
- **Project repositories** for experiments, tools, and implementation work
- **Documentation** that explains the thinking behind each build
- **Work in progress** as new ideas are developed in the open

## Featured focus

Most of the organization’s work lives in the case-study repositories. If you’re visiting for the first time, start there to get the clearest picture of the problems being explored, the decisions behind each project, and the final outcomes.

## Why this exists

This space is meant to make the work easier to browse, understand, and reference. Instead of scattering projects across disconnected repos, Mirabelle Workspace provides a more intentional landing page for:

- showcasing completed work
- organizing research and experiments
- highlighting process as much as results
- creating a clear entry point for collaborators, clients, or curious visitors
